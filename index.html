Privacy Policy — PaperPlanStudioStore

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

Overview

PaperPlanStudioStore Auto-Pins ("App") is a tool designed to help content creators schedule pins to Pinterest from their Etsy product listings. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

  • Pinterest Account Information: Your Pinterest Business account ID and board names (to schedule pins)
  • Etsy Product Data: Product titles, descriptions, and links from your Etsy shop (to create pins)
  • Usage Data: When and how often the app is used (for service improvement)

How We Use Your Data

  • Primary Purpose: To automatically schedule pins to your Pinterest boards
  • Service Improvement: To track performance and fix bugs
  • No Selling: We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties

Data Security

  • All credentials are encrypted
  • We do not store your Pinterest or Etsy passwords
  • API tokens are stored securely and rotated regularly

Third-Party Services

This App integrates with:

  • Pinterest: For pin scheduling (subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy)
  • Etsy: For product data retrieval (subject to Etsy's Privacy Policy)

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time. The App will no longer access your Pinterest or Etsy accounts.
